# Home Page Specification

## Overview
- Target files: `src/blocks/hero.tsx`, `src/blocks/features.tsx`, `src/blocks/workflow.tsx`, `src/blocks/cta.tsx`, `src/blocks/faq.tsx`, `src/blocks/footer.tsx`
- Screenshot: `docs/design-references/lyricedits.ai/home-desktop.png`
- Interaction model: mostly static, videos autoplay, FAQ click-driven.

## Structure
- Hero centered with upload card.
- Gray featured-video section with 3x2 video grid.
- Editorial white section with oversized heavy text and quote.
- Product gray section with left copy and editor preview.
- Stats cards and export tool section.
- Try-free CTA, FAQ, yellow CTA band, footer.

## Styles
- H1: about 56-64px desktop, heavy 800/900, black, line-height near 1.05.
- Accent words: yellow/orange.
- Body text: slate `rgb(100, 116, 139)`, 16px.
- Section padding: 72-120px desktop.
- Cards: white, border `rgb(226, 232, 240)`, radius 20-24px.

## Responsive
- Mobile stacks cards and video grid; heading about 42px; upload card width full.
