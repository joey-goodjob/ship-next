# Pricing Page Specification

## Overview
- Target file: `src/blocks/pricing.tsx`
- Screenshot: `docs/design-references/lyricedits.ai/pricing-desktop.png`
- Interaction model: visual segmented control, plan buttons, FAQ accordion.

## Structure
- Centered pricing hero.
- Four plan cards, with Pro highlighted by yellow outer wrapper and "Most Popular" strip.
- Add credits card with credit selector and purchase summary.
- Credit calculator card with controls left and estimate right.
- Credit info card with grouped rows.
- Enterprise two-column pitch + quote card.

## Styles
- Plan card width: four-column desktop grid.
- Card radius: about 24px.
- Featured card: yellow border/fill outside, white inner body.
- Buttons: yellow primary or white bordered.
- Background: white top; lower FAQ band light gray.

## Responsive
- Mobile stacks plan cards and large info cards into one column.
