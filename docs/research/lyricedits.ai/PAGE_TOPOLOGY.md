## Global Structure
- Header: sticky-looking top nav, 72px tall, white background, logo left, nav/actions right.
- Visual language: white background, light gray section bands, black heavy headings, amber/yellow accent, slate-muted body text.
- Font extraction: `SUSE, sans-serif` for most UI, `Playwrite CU` cursive for the handwritten footer line.
- Primary accent: yellow/orange around `rgb(251, 191, 36)`.
- Card styling: thin `rgb(226, 232, 240)` border, 20-24px radius, white fill, restrained shadows.

## Home Page Order
1. Header/Nav: logo, Pricing, Contact, theme icon, Log in, Sign up.
2. Hero: centered H1, yellow inline emphasis, small value prop line, upload panel.
3. Benefit row: three columns for free preview, customization, languages.
4. Featured videos: gray band, centered title, 3x2 video grid with captions, upload CTA.
5. Editorial pain section: oversized block heading, body copy, quote, two-column rationale.
6. Creativity CTA: centered headline with yellow emphasis and upload button.
7. Product explanation: gray band, text left and editor preview/mock video right.
8. Stats: three metric cards.
9. Editor exports: gray band, text left, NLE icons right.
10. Try free: centered icon features and upload CTA.
11. FAQ: gray band, left intro and right accordion.
12. Yellow CTA band: short music-facing copy and large upload button.
13. Footer: handwritten tagline, featured badge, logo/socials, multi-column links.

## Pricing Page Order
1. Header/Nav.
2. Pricing hero: centered title, subtitle, monthly/annual segmented control.
3. Plan cards: Free, Creator, Pro highlighted, Ultra.
4. Credit pack card: credit quantity selector and purchase summary.
5. Credits calculator: controls on left, estimated credits on right.
6. Credit info table: grouped model costs.
7. Enterprise section: left pitch, right card.
8. FAQ: centered heading and accordion.
9. Yellow contact CTA.
10. Footer.

## Responsive Notes
- Desktop uses max-width containers around 1200px.
- Mobile stacks all grids to one column, with nav reduced and CTA buttons full-width or centered.
- Large headings reduce from 56-64px desktop to about 40-44px mobile.
