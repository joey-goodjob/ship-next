## Known Differences
- The original LyricEdits logo and exact brand assets are intentionally replaced with LyricVideo AI.
- Source webm examples are referenced remotely to preserve the original video-card behavior without storing large files in the repo.
- The product editor screenshot is recreated as a CSS mockup rather than copied as a raster image, because no standalone editor screenshot asset was exposed in the extracted asset list.
- Font is matched through local/system sans fallback rather than bundling the exact source site's SUSE webfont.
